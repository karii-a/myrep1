Hello, I'm Kareena!

I'm a frontend developer and UI/UX design enthusiast based in Kathmandu, Nepal. I build web experiences that feel intuitive, look beautiful, and mean something — blending art, technology, and ethics in every project I take on.

My journey started with curiosity: why do some websites feel effortless while others feel like a chore? That question led me to study design principles, dive deep into code, and never stop asking "how can this be better?"

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring Kathmandu's hidden cafés, sketching interface ideas, or scrolling design inspiration at 2 AM.
